Toluca, Mexico State

Toluca is the city where I live, and its located in Mexico State, very near to Mexico City. The cosmovrital is a botanical garden housed in a former market building. It is famous for its massive stained-glass mural, created by artist Leopoldo Flores. The mural, one of the largest of its kind in the world, represents the evolution of life, combining themes of nature, science, and the cosmos.
